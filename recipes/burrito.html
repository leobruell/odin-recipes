Burrito

Description

Burritos are a delicious food source. This recipe will describe how to cook the classic bean and cheese burrito. This burrito is best if microwaved first, but it can be eaten cold if no warming machine is available.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Can of refried beans

Steps

  1. Place tortilla on plate
  2. Take refried beans from can and spread evenly across middle of tortilla
  3. Sprinkle shredded cheese on tortilla
  4. Microwave for about 30 seconds
  5. Eat burrito yum yum!
