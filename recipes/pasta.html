Pasta

Description

Pasta is a healthy and yummy dish. It is best for lunch or dinner, but can really be eaten at any meal. It is highly customizable as there are a wide selection of pasta shapes to choose from. Additionally, there are many different sauces available, including tomato, pesto, and alfredo.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pasta sauce of your choice

Steps

  1. Heat pot of water to boil
  2. Pour pasta into pot
  3. Wait around 10 minutes
  4. Drain pasta
  5. Pour sauce on pasta
  6. Eat pasta yum yum!
